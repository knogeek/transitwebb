Since the announcement of Quarantine in March our business is up by 15%. Most days we don't stop rolling until the shift is over. For this reason we can't accept reservations or requests for a specific driver.

Rules

Please keep in mind that you are riding in a private vehicle used by the public. To ensure a safe and pleasant ride we ask the following of our riders:

  1. Masks or face coverings are required for both the rider and driver.
  2. The right front seat is not available to riders.
  3. The safest place to sit and respect social distancing is the right, second row seat. The third row seats two people if you have more than three people with you. Seating capacity is reduced to five people while social distancing rules are in place.
  4. Take your trash with you
  5. Avoid doing anything before the ride that would create an offensive smell for the passengers that follow you. If you stink then we will end the ride early.
  6. A car seat is required for children under the age of 7. A rear facing car seat is required for children under the age of two.
  7. Our cars are non-smoking cars
  8. Please resist the urge to eat or drink during your ride.
  9. We cannot allow any open containers.
  10. If you are under 18 you must be accompanied by an adult.
  11. Buckle Up for Safety!
  12. How risky/deadly is COVID-19?

Not as risky or as deadly as we feared. As of 9-14-2020 COVID-19 has infected about 7.5% of the 21% of Virginia that has been tested. Only 2% of those who tested positive died.
